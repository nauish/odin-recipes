Pan Seared Steak

Description

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlics

Steps

  1. Dry – use paper towels to pat the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter.
  2. Season generously – just before cooking steaks, sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat the pan on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.
  4. Butter and aromatics – melt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over steaks and cook to your desired doneness.
  5. Remove steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain.
